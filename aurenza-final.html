About the Studio

We design spaces that breathe with intention

Aurenza Studio was founded on the belief that interior design is not decoration — it is the architecture of feeling. Every room we touch is an opportunity to create an environment that elevates how you live.

Our approach combines rigorous spatial thinking with a deep sensitivity to materials, light, and the invisible rhythms of daily life.

A clear, collaborative process designed to make designing your space feel effortless — not overwhelming.

Common Questions

Things people
often ask

Every project starts with conversation. Here are the questions we hear most from new clients — answered honestly.

Ask Us Directly →
  • How does your design process work?+
    We start with a free 30-minute discovery call, then develop concept directions, present mood boards and material palettes, move through detailed design and procurement, and finish with on-site installation. You're involved at every key decision point.
  • What does it cost to work with you?+
    Our design fees are structured on project scope. Residential projects typically start from ₹2,500/sq ft (design + execution). We provide a detailed, transparent quote after the discovery call — no surprises, ever.
  • How long does a full project take?+
    A standard residential project takes 4–8 months from initial concept to final installation. We give you a clear timeline with milestones in our proposal, and we stick to it.
  • Do you work on projects outside Bangalore?+
    Absolutely. We've completed projects in Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Chennai. Travel and coordination costs are discussed upfront — nothing hidden.
  • Can I see the design before execution starts?+
    Yes — always. We provide detailed 3D visualisations, material samples, and a full specification sheet before a single nail goes in. You approve, we execute.
  • Do you offer just design, or full execution too?+
    Both. We offer design-only packages for clients who have their own contractors, or full design-and-build packages where we manage everything end-to-end. You choose the level of involvement you need.
